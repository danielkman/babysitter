## Label Taxonomy

- Apply labels that reflect the change, not the aspirational status. Use `type:*` (feat/fix/chore/docs/refactor) and `area:*` (package or surface) labels when the repo defines them.
- Do not invent new labels on the fly. If the existing taxonomy does not fit, surface that as a question rather than creating one-off labels.
- Priority/severity labels (`p0`, `p1`, `sev:high`) are owned by humans — do not self-assign them from an AI agent context.
